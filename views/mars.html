Mars

According to the NASA website, Mars is half the size of Earth. It is a cold planet yet red in color but from the rusty iron in the ground. Mars also has volcanoes, canyons, and polar ice caps however, most similarly to Earth, Mars has seasons and weather. Unfortunately, Mars does not have water as Earth does. There are signs that floods happened once upon a time but most water now lives in icy dirt and thin clouds, with some evidence of salty water in the ground.
