According to the NASA website, Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system, it’s only a little bigger than Earth’s moon. It is a rocky planet with a solid surface covered in craters. Mercury is the closest planet to the sun yet it is not the hottest. A day on Mercury is about 59 days on Earth, however, a year is only about 88 days!
