According to the NASA website, Venus is the hottest planet in our solar system despite not being the closest to the sun. It is a little bigger than Earth and also has mountains and volcanoes. However, Venus spins in the opposite direction of Earth and is very slow. A day on Venus is 243 days on Earth yet a year is only 225 Earth days! Can you imagine your year being less than your day?
